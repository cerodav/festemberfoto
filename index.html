Introduction

Festember 2015 presents you Festember Photoraphy Contest.

Preliminary round

There will be one sole eliminative preliminary round for the event. Participants can submit a maximum of 3 photographs for the round on any theme. All photographs must be submitted at the same time. The photos will then be uploaded on the Festember page. The judges will pick 30 finalists from the preliminary stage.

Final round

Three non-eliminative rounds will be conducted. Finalists are required to submit only ONE photograph for each round of the finals. The topic of each round will be announced after the deadline of the previous round. The marks scored by the participant in each round will be added. The total marks obtained will be the sole judging criteria

Prizes worth 60,000 INR will be awarded under the following categories.


Special Prize

For one participant getting the maximum number of likes during the preliminary round.

Judges’ prize

For the participants selected by our Judges in the finals. The top 3 contestants with themaximum marks at the end of three rounds will be awarded prizes in the following order:Winner, First Runner-up, Second Runner-up

Viewer’s prize

The photographs submitted for the final rounds shall be exhibited during Festember. There will be a voting for the displayed photos. The participant whose photos get the maximum number of votes will receive this prize along with Festember goodies.
